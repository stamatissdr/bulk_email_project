How to Get an App Password for Gmail

  1. Go to your Google Account.
  2. Select Security.
  3. Under "Signing in to Google," select 2-Step Verification.
  4. At the bottom of the page, select App passwords.
  5. Enter a name for the app password (e.g., "Bulk Email Sender").
  6. Select Generate.
  7. Follow the instructions to enter the app password (the 16 character code in the yellow bar) on your device.
  8. Select Done.

Once you've generated the app password, you can use it in the Bulk Email Sender application.
